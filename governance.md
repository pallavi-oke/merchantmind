# Sentinel Governance: Privacy by Design

The "Sentinel Sheriff" is the core of our governance philosophy. In a world of agentic AI, privacy cannot be an afterthought—it must be an architectural constant.

---

### 🏛️ The Three Pillars of Sentinel Governance

#### 1. Strict Roster Interception
The system runs a sub-second pass on every user query against the complete, strict corporate team roster mapping. Rather than relying on "fuzzy" AI reasoning, Claude evaluates the query explicitly against the manager's authorized team members to catch cross-team PII breaches instantly.

#### 2. Cross-Family Model Audit
Sentinel utilizes a "Polyglot" strategy for high-risk requests. By using **Anthropic Claude Opus 4.5** to audit the output of **Google Gemini 2.5 Pro**, we create an independent verification layer. This prevents "correlated blind spots" where a single model might excuse its own hallucination or privacy lapse.

#### 3. The "Governance Guardrail" (Diplomatic Denial)
The most unique feature of the Sentinel Sheriff is the **Diplomatic Denial**. Instead of a generic "Access Denied" error, the system:
- Acknowledges the privacy restriction.
- Proactively suggests a safe, non-PII alternative.
- Delivers real-time team-level aggregates that satisfy the user's analytical intent without exposing individual data.

### ⚖️ Enforcement Examples

| User Query | Sheriff Action | Governance Response |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| *"How is the overall rewards budget doing?"* | **PASS** | Victoria (Analyst) provides a fiscal burn-rate summary. |
| *"Show me the rewards for Pallavi in Engineering."* | **INTERCEPT** | Mike (Sheriff) blocks PII and provides the Engineering team's total budget instead. |
| *"Who is the highest earner?"* | **BLOCK** | Mike (Sheriff) intervenes to prevent individual employee ranking. |

---

### 📋 Auditability
Every decision made by the Sheriff is logged, including:
- The user query that triggered the intervention.
- The specific rule or model that flagged the risk.
- The alternative response provided to the user.

This creates a defensible audit trail that ensures the platform is compliant with corporate data protection standards.
